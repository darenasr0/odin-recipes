Chorizo Street Tacos

Description

Chorizo and chipotle peppers give these street-style tacos some serious heat.

"I love these tacos," says reviewer Yoly. "But then again I love anything and everything with Mexican Chorizo."

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Dressing
  • Seasoning mix
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the pasta.
  2. Whisk the dressing with the seasoning mix.
  3. Combine the pasta with the vegetables, then toss in the seasoned dressing.
