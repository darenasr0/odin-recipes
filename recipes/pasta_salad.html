Pasta Salad

Description

The pasta salad is a plate which my mum always do every days of summer.

I really like that!! You only need pasta and not much.

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Dressing
  • Seasoning mix
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Cook and drain the pasta.
  2. Whisk the dressing with the seasoning mix.
  3. Combine the pasta with the vegetables, then toss in the seasoned dressing.
